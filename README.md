# 🏥 Hospital Appointment Optimization & Patient Analytics System (HAOPS)

> **An end-to-end Business Analyst portfolio project focused on reducing patient no-shows, improving appointment scheduling, and enabling data-driven decision-making through Business Analysis, SQL, Python, Jira, and Power BI.**

---

# 📌 Project Overview

The **Hospital Appointment Optimization & Patient Analytics System (HAOPS)** is a Business Analyst project developed to analyze hospital appointment data and identify factors contributing to patient no-shows. The project follows the complete **Business Analysis lifecycle**, beginning with requirement gathering and ending with dashboard development and User Acceptance Testing (UAT).

Using healthcare appointment data, the solution provides actionable insights through SQL analysis and interactive Power BI dashboards, enabling hospital administrators to improve operational efficiency and patient experience.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Hospitals often experience operational challenges due to patient no-shows, including:

* Missed appointments resulting in doctor idle time
* Inefficient appointment scheduling
* Poor resource utilization
* Increased operational costs
* Limited visibility into patient attendance patterns
* Difficulty identifying high-risk patients

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

* Reduce patient no-show rates.
* Improve appointment scheduling efficiency.
* Identify key drivers of missed appointments.
* Enable data-driven decision-making.
* Monitor hospital performance using KPIs.
* Improve patient experience and operational efficiency.

# 💡 Key Business Insights

* Attendance Rate: **79.81%**
* Missed Appointment Rate: **20.19%**
* Adult patients recorded the highest number of appointments.
* Longer lead times increased the likelihood of missed appointments.
* Interactive dashboards enable quick identification of no-show patterns and support better scheduling decisions.

---

# 📋 Business Recommendations

* Implement automated SMS reminders for appointments with longer lead times.
* Monitor high-risk patient segments to reduce missed appointments.
* Optimize appointment scheduling using dashboard insights.
* Review no-show trends regularly to improve operational planning.
